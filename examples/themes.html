Built-in Themes

wavesurf ships with two built-in themes: DARK (default) and LIGHT. Both use the circle play button style.

from wavesurf import DARK, LIGHT WaveSurfer(audio=audio, sr=24000, title="DARK Theme", theme=DARK) WaveSurfer(audio=audio, sr=24000, title="LIGHT Theme", theme=LIGHT)
