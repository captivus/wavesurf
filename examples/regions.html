Regions Plugin

The regions plugin lets you highlight and annotate sections of audio. Regions can be dragged, resized, and styled independently.

from wavesurf import Plugins, EventHandler WaveSurfer( audio=audio, sr=24000, title="With Regions", plugins=[Plugins.regions()], on_ready=""" var regions = ws.plugins[0]; regions.addRegion({start: 0.5, end: 1.5, ...}); """, )
